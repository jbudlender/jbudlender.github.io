## SALDRU Weekly Seminar Series

SALDRU hosts a weekly in-person seminar series at the School of Economics at UCT, where local and visiting scholars present research broadly in the fields of Labour and Development Economics. Seminars are generally open to the public; sign up [here](https://www.saldru.uct.ac.za/seminar-mailing-list/) if you would like to be added to the external mailing list.  

I co-convene the 2026 seminar series with [Amy Thornton](https://saldru.uct.ac.za/contacts/amy-thornton). If you would like to present your own research, please [email us](mailto:joshua.budlender@uct.ac.za,amy.thornton@uct.ac.za). While SALDRU does not generally provide funding to present at the weekly seminar series, if your research fits into our thematic areas we can provide an invitation letter with which you can apply for travel or conference funding from your own institution.

## SALDRU-ERSA December Workshop

I am also a co-organizer of the SALDRU-ERSA December Workshop, an annual workshop held each December at UCT, organised by SALDRU and [Economic Research Southern Africa](https://econrsa.org) (ERSA). The aim of the December workshop is to encourage overseas-based researchers to present frontier Economics research in South Africa, as well as provide a venue for local researchers to engage with and present this kind of work.

The 2026 edition of the workshop is scheduled for 14 and 15 December 2026. A call for papers will be released soon.

You can read about the 2025 SALDRU-ERSA December Workshop [here](https://saldru.uct.ac.za/articles/2026-01-28-saldru-and-ersa-co-host-second-edition-annual-december-workshop), which had Taryn Dinkelman and Joe Kaboski presenting keynote lectures ([full programme](https://saldru.uct.ac.za/sites/default/files/media/documents/saldru_uct_ac_za/2760/programme-v1.pdf)).

The [inaugural edition of the workshop](https://saldru.uct.ac.za/articles/2025-02-04-inaugural-edition-saldru-december-workshop) was held in December 2024, with Dennis Egger as the keynote speaker.
